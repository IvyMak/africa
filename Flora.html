Culture

Home to 54 beautiful countries, Africa is known for its vibrant and diverse array of culture, expressed through arts, clothes, food, music and so much more!

Art

Africa's arts scene is composed mostly of scupltures, pottery, jewellery and beading. Depending on the region the art has orignated, the charcteristics will represent distinct values, beliefs and customs. Common themes that are found include women, childen, war, animals, and religion. Art is sometimes used in the form of masks or sculptures for traditional ceremonies to honour deities or ancestors.


Music

Africa's music scene is known for its diversity. It is often rooted and centred on drum and singing. Other African musical traditions invlolve strings and horns. Music is a very important part of life in Africa, with its influence speading worldwide. It is often used in rites and ceromnies, performed by specialist drummers and court musicians.


Clothes

Clothing is an important part of tradition in Africa. The fabric and prints used often indicates the different tribes throughout the continent and is a reflection of the status of individuals or groups within each community. The majority of African clothes are vibrant and colourful.
