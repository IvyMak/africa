Mombasa, Kenya


Mombasa is home to the biggest East African port ,which made it a regional cultural hub, so expect influential reflections of India, Arabia and Africa.
Travellers tip: For great food, where the local crowds are will usually be your best bet.

Lagos, Nigeria


One of the fastest growing cities in Africa, Lagos hustle and bustle is sure to keep you busy; even you night owls.
Fun Fact:It's been said that "if Lagos was a country, it would be the 5th largest economy in Africa".

Makgadikgadi Salt Pans, Botswana


A UNESCO world heritage site, the Makgadikgadi salt pans are one of the biggest salt pan in the world.
Fun fact: The region it is located in was recently pinpointed as where modern humans came to be!

Marrakech, Morocco


A top destination in Marrakech,the souks have been decribed as a " magpie's nest of treasures that is manna for shop-til-you-drop fanatics" by the Lonely Planet .
Travellers trip: dont go to the first shop you see, have a wonder around to find the best deals for you.
